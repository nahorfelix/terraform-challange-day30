# Terraform Challenge Day 30

Day 30 is the final preparation day: final simulated exam, fill-in-the-blank precision review, readiness check, exam logistics confirmation, and 30-day reflection.

## Structure

- `day30-final-prep/docs/` for submission and evidence
- `day30-final-prep/logs/` for timing and quick notes
- `day30-final-prep/review/` for fill-in-the-blank and readiness answers

## Workflow

1. Take Practice Exam 5 and log score.
2. Complete 20 fill-in-the-blank questions.
3. Answer final readiness questions (cold, no notes).
4. Confirm exam-day logistics checklist.
5. Write final 30-day reflection and submission.
